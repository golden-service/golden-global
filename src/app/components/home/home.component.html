What types of paper can be recycled?

We Do

There are three different types of paper that can be recycled; These are known as ‘mill broke’ – this is essentially any paper scrap or trimmings that come from the manufacturing of paper, this generally gets recycled internally by the paper mills. There is also ‘pre-consumer waste’. This is paper that has been sent away from the paper mill but then discarded prior to being used by a consumer. Finally, there is ‘post-consumer waste’ – this is any paper material that has been received and used by a consumer and then discarded, such as newspapers.
